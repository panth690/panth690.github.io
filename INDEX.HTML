What is Hinduism?

Hinduism is one of the world's oldest religions, rooted in the spiritual practices of the Indian subcontinent. It embraces a wide range of beliefs, rituals, and philosophies.

Bhagavad Gita: Song of God

The Bhagavad Gita is a sacred text in the form of a dialogue between Lord Krishna and Arjuna. It offers guidance on life, duty, and devotion.

Diwali: Festival of Lights

Diwali, also known as Deepavali, celebrates the triumph of light over darkness. Homes are lit with diyas, and prayers are offered to Goddess Lakshmi.
